CV

Hi! I'm a mathemetician interested in topics in differential geometry and quantum field theory. Currently I am working on problems concerning character varieties of surfaces, in particular Hitchin components and their compactifications.

Currently, I am a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences in Leipzig. I got my PhD in summer 2024 from UT Austin, and in Fall of 2025 I will begin a postdoc at Yale.

Preprints

  • Fock Bundles and Hitchin Components (joint with Alex Thomas and Georgios Kydonakis)

    We define a correspondence between higher complex structures and real representations, and show that we get a map in a neighborhood of the Fuchsian locus.

  • Limits of Convex Projective Surfaces and Finsler Metrics

    The dots in the picture below are elements of the 444 triangle reflection group of length at most 20. The x and y axes are the logarithms of the top and botton eigenvalues of these elements under SL(3,R) Hitchin representations. You can see the dots collecting onto a lattice as the representation goes to infinity. This paper gives a geometric way of telling which lattice point a group element will limit to.

  • Symmetries of 2d TQFTs and Equivariant Verlinde Formulae for General Groups, (joint with Ali Shehper, Du Pei, and Sergei Gukov)

    We use the concept of symmetry gauging to calculate equivariant Verlinde formulas for non simply connected groups.
