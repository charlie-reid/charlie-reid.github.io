I am PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin interested in differential geometry and quantum field theory. I will be a visiting graduate fellow at Harvard for the 2023-2024 academic year, and will be applying to post-doc jobs this fall.

Currently, I'm working two general problems concerning character varieties of surfaces. One is to identifying Hitchin components moduli spaces of complex analytic objects, in a mapping class group equivariant way that generalizes the uniformization theorem for Riemann surfaces of genus at least 2. The other is to realize tropical boundaries of Hitchin components as moduli spaces of geometric objects, in a way that generalizes Thurston's compactification of Teichmuller space.

  • Limits of Convex Projective Surfaces and Finsler Metrics, in preparation

    Below is a picture of the Jordan projections of the elements of the 444 triangle reflection group of length at most 20 as one traverses the SL(3,R) Hitchin component. You can see the Jordan projections collecting onto a lattice as the representation goes to infinity.
